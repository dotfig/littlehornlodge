About

UPDATED Home/Cabin/Lodge: Newer Windows, Roof, Siding, Well, Remodeled Interior. Currently used as a lodge/retreat, but this was previously a Home Residence. Only 1/3 Acre to maintain, but located on S.G.L. 54, featuring 23000+ acres out the back door. Additional 20,000 acres (SGL 44) connects to SGL 54, so lots of great area! Excellent Hunting - Deer, Turkey, Bear, Small Game, Fur. Nearby Clarion River, Buzzard Swamp allows for great fishing and recreation. Endless mountains/hiking/outdoor opportunities. Many Amenities are available to purchase (Cub Cadet, Kayaks, etc). Current Satellite-Dish Network. indoor plumbing, Boiler Furnace heat in main home, Propane heat in front addition. Well water for cleaning, bathing, etc. We use a Polar Water Cooler for drinking water. Quiet area. Convenient location allowing for easy Winter access. Rte 949 is a state maintained road, plowed by PaDOT. Shed on property is 12' x 16' - lots of room for storage. Attic area in home as well. Located 2-1/4 hours from Pittsburgh, Convenient access from I80. Nearby Cities: Brookville 16 Miles / Dubois 16 miles / Ridgway 16 miles / Brockway 9 miles.
